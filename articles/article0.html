Lake Velence

1/3

Lake Velence is a beautiful and cosy lake 50 kilometers from Budapest. It can be a great alternative to Balaton as it is cheaper and less crowded.

By car it is only 30 minutes to reach Agárd (my personal favorite town) but if you prefer train it is also only an hour or so. If you don't mind if the beach is a bit more crowded Velence is also a good option. It has more facilities but a bit more expensive.

There are different options to get down to the beach. In Agárd there are two beaches you have to pay for and a free one. On the free beach you may also rent SUP, surf and pedalo. In the VVSI sports center you may also rent a kayak or a sailboat. That place is my personal recommendation if you want to spend your time on a paying beach.

There are different options for a good lunch (apart from your own sandwich) depending on your budget. My personal favorites are Csutora and Szunyogsziget Halászcsdárda. This two are in the moderate price category and offers great Hungarian food. Csutora runs a more contemporary but still meat based cuisine as the other one that is a more traditional one with much more fish on the menu. The other cool thing is that you may take a nice boat trip to the north side of the lake to eat there.

I would like to mention that there is a good cycling root around the lake, approximately 28 kilometers, so if you are more into that kind of fun it can be a great one-day cycling trip. Don't forget to stop by at the viewpoint near the south-west end of the lake at the bird reserve and the Pákozd memorial site where you can see the whole lake at once. An absolutely spectacular view.
